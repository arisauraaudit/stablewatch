## Monetization Thinking (to be updated as users join)

**Working hypothesis:**
The free tier (public channel, all alerts) is the hook.
A paid tier works if users start relying on alerts for real decisions.

**Proposed paid experiment (draft — propose when ≥ 3 active users):**
- "Pro tier" — $5–10 CAD/month
- Value adds: priority DM alerts (faster than channel), more stablecoins, weekly depeg summary report
- Distribution: direct ask to the most engaged free users

**Who would pay — criteria:**
- Uses the alerts to inform any real position (even small)
- Has commented, shared, or asked questions about the bot
- Mentioned they "would pay for X" unprompted

**Target: 1–3 people for first paid ask.**
Names/handles to be filled in as users engage.
